#4 Exquisite Corpse

We together as a group of three (My group member: Elina Jing & Pranav Chaparala), created this 'Exquisite Corpse'. Each of us needed to draw a figure with three components: the head, upper body, and lower body. We exchanged different body parts with our peers, and transferred the exquisite corpse drawing into code. My drawing is: Elina's head part + Pranav's upper body part + My lower body part.
